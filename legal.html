Overview

This page brings together the legal information for the AquaSave website, deployed frontend app, and future connected device services. Some app flows currently preview the experience while backend and ESP32 integrations are completed.

By using AquaSave you accept the terms below. If you do not agree with any part of them, please stop using the service. These terms cover website visitors and people who use the app to create garden records, configure thresholds, review weather guidance, log watering activity, or later connect a device.

Plain language first

We write for humans. Legalese only when it is strictly required.

Yours to keep

You can request a copy of these terms at any time through the contact form.

Updated when needed

We notify users of significant changes through the website, app, or email when those channels are available.

02

Terms & Conditions

These terms govern your relationship with EcoDrop, the company that builds and operates AquaSave. They describe permitted use of the website and frontend app, the current limits of connected features, and the responsibilities that will apply as backend and device services are enabled.

Eligibility

You must be at least 16 years old, or the minimum digital-consent age in your country, to create an account. If you use AquaSave on behalf of a household or organization, you confirm you are authorized to accept these terms for them.

Account responsibility

You are responsible for keeping your credentials safe and for any activity that happens through your account. Tell us as soon as possible if you suspect unauthorized access. We may suspend sessions if we detect unusual behavior.

Service availability

AquaSave is delivered on a best-effort basis. The current app may include frontend-only or beta flows while connected device features are being integrated. Treat app recommendations as decision support, not as a guarantee of physical watering.

Plans & access

If paid plans are enabled later, charges will follow the cycle you select. Until then, plan and access information in the app should be treated as informational.

03

Acceptable use

AquaSave is built to support irrigation decisions for domestic gardens, urban horticulture and small growing areas. We ask that you use it responsibly and avoid behaviors that put other people, the service, devices, or shared infrastructure at risk.

  • Do not attempt to disrupt the service, reverse engineer the firmware, or extract data from accounts that are not yours.
  • Do not use the device or the API to control irrigation systems you are not authorized to operate.
  • Do not upload content that is unlawful, harassing, or that infringes someone else's rights.
  • Do not use AquaSave to circumvent local water-use restrictions or regulations.

If we believe your usage breaks these rules, we may pause access temporarily while we look into it. We will reach out so you can clarify and recover access whenever possible.

04

Privacy Policy

Our privacy approach is simple: collect the minimum required to run the service, keep it safe, and never sell it. This section describes the data we handle and why.

What we collect

  • Account data: username, email when provided, language preference, theme choice, and profile settings.
  • Garden and device records: garden names, locations, plant counts, plant types, descriptions, thresholds, schedules and device status shown in the app.
  • Watering and sensor-style data: soil moisture, temperature, weather context, pump tank values, watering events and manual history entries when entered or received.
  • Support data: messages and attachments you send through the contact form.
  • Technical data: limited diagnostics (browser, OS, error logs) used to keep the service stable.

Why we use it

We use this data to run the app experience, show dashboards, generate watering recommendations, prepare device setup flows, support users and improve irrigation logic. We never use garden or sensor data to build advertising profiles.

How long we keep it

During the frontend stage, much of the information may live locally or in test flows. Once remote services are enabled, account and device data will be kept only as long as needed to provide the service and meet legal obligations.

05

Security & data protection

Keeping your information safe is something we take seriously. The deployed frontend already separates user-facing flows from future service integrations, and production data services will apply security controls as backend features go live.

  • Secure transport: production connections between the app, services and device APIs should use encrypted channels.
  • Protected storage: sensitive account and device data will be protected when remote persistence is enabled.
  • Limited access: only authorized team members or services should access operational data needed to support AquaSave.
  • Recovery practices: production backend services will include backup and recovery procedures appropriate for the data stored.
  • Independent reviews: we welcome responsible disclosure of security issues and review our setup with external partners on a regular cadence.

If you find what looks like a security issue, please reach out via the contact form so we can investigate and credit your finding when appropriate.

06

Your data & rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. AquaSave honors these rights regardless of jurisdiction; we believe they are simply good practice.

  • Access & export: download a copy of your data in a portable format from your account settings.
  • Correction: update incorrect details directly in the app or through the contact form.
  • Deletion: delete your account and the data associated with it, except where law requires us to keep records.
  • Object & restrict: ask us to stop or limit specific types of processing.
  • Complaint: file a complaint with your local data protection authority if you think we are not handling things correctly.
07

Children's privacy

AquaSave is not designed for children. Accounts are intended for adults and adolescents at or above the digital-consent age that applies in their country (16 in many places, lower in some jurisdictions).

If we learn that a younger person has created an account without proper consent, we will close the account and delete the associated personal data. Parents or guardians who believe a child has provided personal information without authorization can reach us via the contact form to request deletion.

Of course, families regularly use AquaSave together, and that is encouraged. Children can watch the garden, learn about water care, and help schedule cycles, as long as they do so through an adult's account.

08

Cookies

AquaSave uses a minimal number of cookies and similar technologies. We try to keep this list as short as possible.

Functional Remember UI choices like theme or dashboard layout. 12 months

09

Third-party services

To run AquaSave reliably we lean on a small set of trusted partners. These providers process specific pieces of data strictly to deliver their part of the service, under contracts that match the privacy commitments we make to you.

  • Hosting & infrastructure: cloud providers host the website and app and will support backend services when enabled.
  • Authentication and messaging: services may help with account access, password recovery and service notifications when those features are connected.
  • Payments: if paid plans are introduced, PCI-compliant processors will handle payment details.
  • Weather and location data: forecast and geocoding providers help the app show local weather and irrigation guidance.
  • Anonymous analytics: privacy-respecting analytics that help us understand product usage in aggregate, with no personal profiling.

When data leaves the region you live in, we use the legal frameworks recognized by your jurisdiction (such as standard contractual clauses) so your data remains protected on the way and wherever it lands.

11

Beta & experimental features

Some AquaSave features may appear as beta, preview or frontend-only flows while backend, firmware and ESP32 connectivity are completed. These features help validate the experience before full release.

  • Preview features may simulate a flow, change their interface, or be replaced as the connected service evolves.
  • We may collect feedback or limited diagnostics to understand whether the app flow is clear and reliable.
  • Preview features do not guarantee physical irrigation or remote persistence until the related backend and device integrations are active.

Feedback is genuinely appreciated. If something feels off in a beta feature, tell us through the contact form so we can fix it before it ships to everyone.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your email or phone number, and we do not include third-party advertising in our communications.
