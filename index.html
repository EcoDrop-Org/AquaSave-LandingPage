How it works

Three steps to smarter watering.

Set up takes minutes. AquaSave guides you through every step.

01

Connect the device

Place the smart device near your garden, plug it in, and pair it with the app via guided setup.

02

Check your data

See live soil moisture, soil temperature, and water flow, at a glance, in clear cards.

03

Automate or take control
